My Proven Process

A transparent, step-by-step approach that ensures your project success from initial consultation to final delivery

1

Initial Consultation

We start with a detailed discussion to understand your project requirements, goals, and vision.

  • Free 30-minute consultation call
  • Project scope and requirements analysis
  • Timeline and budget discussion
  • Questions and clarifications
  • Compatibility assessment
2

Proposal & Agreement

I provide a detailed proposal with clear deliverables, timeline, and transparent pricing.

  • Comprehensive project proposal
  • Detailed scope of work
  • Clear timeline with milestones
  • Transparent pricing breakdown
  • Terms and conditions agreement
3

Research & Planning

Deep dive into your industry, target audience, and competitors to create the best strategy.

  • Industry and competitor research
  • Target audience analysis
  • Content strategy development
  • Technical requirements planning
  • Project roadmap creation
4

Development & Creation

The actual work begins with regular updates and milestone check-ins to ensure we're on track.

  • Active development/writing phase
  • Regular progress updates
  • Milestone deliveries
  • Quality assurance testing
  • Client feedback incorporation
5

Review & Refinement

Collaborative review process with up to 2 revision rounds to ensure everything meets your expectations.

  • Initial draft/version presentation
  • Client feedback collection
  • First revision round
  • Second revision round (if needed)
  • Final approval process
6

Delivery & Support

Final delivery with documentation, training, and ongoing support to ensure your success.

  • Final project delivery
  • Complete documentation
  • Training and handover
  • 30-day post-delivery support
  • Future collaboration discussion
