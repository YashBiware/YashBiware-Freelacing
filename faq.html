Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer? +
I offer three core services: Website Development (responsive design, SEO optimization, ReactJS development), Content Writing (blog articles, SEO content, website copy), and Content Polishing (proofreading, tone adjustment, quality assurance). Each service is tailored to meet your specific business needs.
What is your typical response time? +
I maintain professional response times of 2-4 hours via WhatsApp and 24 hours via email. For urgent project matters, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach me. I believe in clear, consistent communication throughout every project.
How do you price your services? +
Pricing is project-specific and depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. I provide detailed quotes with transparent pricing - no hidden fees. Each quote includes project scope, deliverables, timeline, and payment terms. Contact me with your project details for a custom quote.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes! Every project includes two complimentary revision rounds. This ensures your complete satisfaction with the final deliverable. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are available at a reasonable rate if needed.
What is your typical project timeline? +
Timelines vary by project scope: Simple websites (3-7 days), Complex websites (1-3 weeks), Content writing (2-5 days per 1000 words), Content polishing (1-3 days). I provide realistic timelines upfront and keep you updated on progress throughout the project.
Do you work with international clients? +
Absolutely! I work with clients globally. Communication is primarily through WhatsApp and email, making it easy to collaborate across time zones. I'm flexible with meeting times and ensure smooth project coordination regardless of location.
What technologies do you use for web development? +
I specialize in modern web technologies: HTML5, CSS3 (Grid, Flexbox, Custom Properties), JavaScript (ES6+), ReactJS, responsive design frameworks, and SEO optimization tools. I stay updated with the latest web standards and best practices.
Can you help with SEO optimization? +
Yes! SEO optimization is integrated into both my web development and content writing services. This includes proper meta tags, structured data, fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, keyword-optimized content, and technical SEO best practices.
What makes you different from other freelancers? +
As a dedicated solo freelancer, you get direct access to me throughout your project - no middlemen or communication gaps. I focus on transparent pricing, reliable communication, quality deliverables, and building long-term partnerships. Your success is my success.
